L2-Norm

Mathematics 路 Data Minig ETH 路 2016-01-08 路 Lukas Woodtli

A vector \(\vec a\) has the \(L_2\)-Norm:

$$\norm{\vec a}_{2} = sqrt(sum_(k=1)^n|a_k|^2)$$
$$n$$

: Dimensions

$$a_k$$

: \(k\)-th element of \(a\)
